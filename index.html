Hi! I am a Ph.D. student in the Computer Science Department at Xi'an Jiaotong University (expected to graduate in 2018).
Now, I join dumontierlab Lab at Stanford University as a Visiting Student Researcher, working with Prof. Michel Dumontier and Prof. Wei Hu.
I am advised by Prof. Jun Liu and work in the Knowledge Mining Group.

My main research interests lie within Truth Discovery for Linked Data and Processing SPARQL queries over distributed RDF Konwledge Graphs, and I am particularly interested in the intersection between Knowledge Graphs and Deep Learning.
